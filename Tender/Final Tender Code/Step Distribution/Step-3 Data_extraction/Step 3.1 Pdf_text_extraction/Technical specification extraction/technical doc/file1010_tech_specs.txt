               Technical Specification for  Cavitron  Ultrasonic Surgical Aspirator (CUSA) 
Technical Details: 
1. The System should have expanded selection of handpieces and surgical tips which allows the surgeon to 
select the most appropriate combination based on the surgical procedure. 
2. Suction Pump, Integrated, Adjustable upto 600 mg Hg. 
3. The system should be based on 4 major subsystems like Fragmentation, Cooling, Irrigation and 
Aspiration having user-friendly console, color coded tubing for easy set-up of the system and having 
facilities even for fibrous and calcified lesions. 
4. The system must have a feature of Tissue Select (Patient Safety dedicated control) to differentiate firm 
and tougher tissue barriers with a tactile feedback in the hand piece. 
5. Handpieces of frequency 23-25 KHz and 34-36 KHz  must be provided and should be based on 
Magneto-restrictive technology. Handpieces must be compact and Light weight with 120W of energy. 
6. The system must have a feature which maintains fragmentation capability for tissue removal while 
maximizing selectivity for surgeon control around critical structures and can differentiate tissue barriers 
with tactile feedback in the hand piece. 
7. The system should have Tissue release function (automatic shutting off suction for 2 seconds after 
deactivation of the vibration foot switch to prevent against delicate tissue trauma. 
8. The system should have various types of tips having different length and diameter to use in surgical 
clinical application and all tips must be removed from the handpieces. 
9. Should have maximum tip amplitude up to 350 microns. 
10. Should have a suction pathway external to the handpiece housing to enable fast cleaning and 
eliminating tissue trap. 
11. Console should be light, be mounted on a trolley from the same manufacturer with a foot switch. 
12. The system should have 22 different types of tip to use in different clinical procedures. The handpiece 
tips should be from 1.57mm diameter and up to 2.64mm diameter. The Tips should be autoclavable and 
re-usable. 
13. The hand piece must have a dedicated tip for Calcified fibrous tissue removal which increases the 
fragmentation rate by 50% than any other tip. 
14. All Tips Must have Pre-aspiration Holes for cooling the tip and to avoid misting at the surgical area. 
15. The handpiece should have a helical Bone cutting / sculpting tip with 180 degree cutting / abrasive 
surface to enable greater control and precise work near critical structures. 
16. The hand piece must have a laparoscopic tip about 30cm in length and with reusable extended life and 
autoclavable.  
17. For extended hours during surgeries (i.e., for more than 4 - 8 hours) the handpiece should have a built-
in water cooling system to avoid overheating of the hand piece. 
18. The control panel should have an adjustable viewing angle for better visibility in the Operation Theatre. 
19. The footswitch should have a dual paddle with a linear operation for irrigation and operation of the 
hand piece.
