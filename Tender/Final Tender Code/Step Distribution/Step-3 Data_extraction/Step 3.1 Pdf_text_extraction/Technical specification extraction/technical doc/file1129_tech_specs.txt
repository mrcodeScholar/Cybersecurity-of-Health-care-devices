1. Three Chip High definition Camera System 
The system should be truly Digital HDTV endoscopic video camera. 
The system should have the maximum Resolution of 1920 X 1080 
pixels, progressive scan and the consistent use of 16: 9 formats for Input 
& Output to guarantee genuine HDTV. 
A. The system should have facility of Optical & Digital Zoom lens 
to enhance the quality of Image size & cross specialty usage of 
the camera system, regardless of the telescope used. 
B. USB Port for Capturing FULL HD Videos/ HD Stills in External 
USB drive and direct interface of USB Printer to facilitate direct 
printouts. 
C. System should have facility of controlling additional equipments 
like light source/ insufflators and recording device from the 
camera head.   
D. System should have facility to offer various visualization modes 
for surgery and diagnosis by shifting the color spectrum like 
BLUE & GREEN light for recognition of the finest tissue 
Structures and their differentiation.  
E. Parallel live display of visualization modes besides white light 
mode (picture-in-picture). 
 
Modular design: Digital FULL HD camera module should be compatible 
for use with video flexible GI endoscopes.
