Technical Specifications for Near Infrared Spectroscopy. 
 
For brain function, regional oximetry, oxygen reserve index, continuous 
Haemoglobin, fluid responsiveness monitoring features.  
 
1. Brain function monitoring with 4 channel real time EEG simultaneously, 
display of density spectral array representing EEG power and Bi Hemispheric 
activity, continuous numerical display of suppression ratio, artifacts, spectral 
edge frequency, (Right and left)  
2. Regional oximetry monitoring using near infrared spectroscopy and reflectance 
pulse oximetry, differential analysis of regional to arterial oxygen saturation for 
reference SpO2, numerical display of regional oximetry (rSO2), O3, Spo2, base,

3. Non invasive continuous monitoring of oxygen reserve index (ORI), trend 
display of ORI and alarm facility. 
4. Continuous non invasive haemoglobin Spo2, pulse rate, perfusion index, pleth 
variability and oxygen reserve index through same single finger sensor, selectable 
Spo2 averaging modes and sensitivity, display of haemoglobin trend. 
5. Multi touch backlist 10 TFT LCD colour display. 
6. Adjustable brightness and with high resolution.  
7. Integrated detachable hand held monitor for use as a portable independent 
monitor with inbuilt battery backup. 
8. Rotational colour touch screen. 
9. Facility to customize display to see parameter, waveforms or trends. 
10. Trend function with facility to select one or two parameters and more, expand 
or collapse parameter trend for real time analysis.  
11. Facility to store patient data and display trend.
