PCNL Set - 
Infant 
The nephroscope system is required for PCNL in children 0-12 years 
of age. The system needs to have following technical specifications: 
1. It should be suitable for children and adolescents. 
2. It should have panoview operating telescope with offset 
eyepiece having diameter 12 Fr. 
3. The scope should have rod lens operating system. 
4. The eyepiece should be offset to let the working channel 
straight 6-15 degree of view. 
5. The working channel should be 6-7Fr. 
6. The working channel should have a mechanism to prevent 
backflow of irrigation fluid. 
7. It should have 200-250 mm working length. 
8. It should have two operating sheaths, on each of 15 Fr and 18 
Fr with irrigation channel. 
9. It should have hollow obturator/ dilator cannula (2) compatible 
with each of above sheaths. 
10. It should have a telescopic metal dilator set; 9-30 Fr. 
11. The Amplatz sheath should have working sheath 14, 16 and 18 
Fr diameter (one each), round or oval, with irrigation channel. 
12. The working length of the sheath should be 200 – 210 mm. 
13. It should have one step dilator of 12 Fr, working length of 
more than 220 mm - 1 Nos 
14. It should have one step dilator of 15 Fr, working length of 
more than 220 mm -  1 Nos 
15. Initial puncture needle/ cannula for localization of renal 
calyces  - 5 Nos 
16. Guidewire for introduction through initial puncture cannula - 
10 Nos 
17. It should have compatible rigid toothed stone grasping forceps, 
working length of 350 – 400 mm. - 2 Nos 
  
 
18. It should have compatible rigid three pronged stone grasper, 
working length 350 – 400 mm  
19. It should have compatible rigid grasping forceps with alligator 
jaws, working length 350-450 mm.  -  2 Nos  
 
     
20. It should have spare inserts for above forceps  - 3 Nos 
 
21. Instrument container for storage, transport and sterilization. 
22. It should be US FDA/ European CE approved. 
23. The facilities for service back up and maintenance should be 
available through qualified service engineers placed in India.  
24. The company must be able to maintain an uptime of the 
equipment of >95% throughout the warranty and AMC period. 
The company must have an inventory of the spares and 
consumables to ensure the same.
