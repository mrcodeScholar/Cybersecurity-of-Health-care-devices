Description of Function  
To the study of Anti-Convulsion and Anti-Epileptic drugs, whether for 
education, screening or manufacturing of drugs  
Technical Specifications  
1. Should provide 50Hz Stimulus Current variable from 0.25mA to 
500 mA through touch panel controls for producing minimal and 
Supra-maximal seizure in small animals  
2. The duration of Stimulus current is variable from 0.1 second to 1 
second in steps of 0.1 second  
Power Supply 230V 50Hz  
3. Should be supplied with corneal electrode pair (different cup size) 
and ear clip pair  
Standards, Safety and Training  
Product should be CE/BIS approved  
Manufacturer should have ISO certification  
User/Technical manual should be supplied  
Experiment data exported to PC or dedicated data acquisition software 
package.  
Bidders are encouraged to arrange for demonstration of their 
equipment if not able to comply with all specification requirement.
