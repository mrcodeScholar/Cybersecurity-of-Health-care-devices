Technical specifications for High frequency ventilator (oscillator) 
 
 
 Ventilator suitable for pediatric age group including new born  
 Modes: dedicated high frequency oscillator  
 The following settings should be permitted:  
 Mean airway pressure: upto 45 cm H20  
 Frequency: upto 15 Hz  
 Amplitude: upto 100  
 Should allow active expiration  
 Heated wire humidifier with servo temperature control 
 Standard patient circuits for all age groups. Circuits and 
tubings should be reusable. Two additional circuits and 
humidifiers should be provided for each age group,  
 Audible and visual alarms for low & high values of respiratory 
parameters, oxygen concentration, and power failure should be 
provided.  
 Battery back up of at least 60 minute.  
 Real time data display of set and measured values: Mean 
airway pressure; amplitude, % inspiratory time, frequency, bias 
flow and piston displacement.  
 Should work on 220- 240 V AC.  
 Device is produced by 1S0 9001 certified manufacturer 
(Certificate to be submitted)  
 Device is safety certified according CE 93/42, FDA 510k or 
equivalent (Certificate to be submitted)
